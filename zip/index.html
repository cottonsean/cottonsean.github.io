ZIP Demographics

Look up a US ZIP for population, income, and housing stats from the Census ACS 5-Year API. Add more ZIPs to compare side-by-side.

Need a Census API key? Here's how (takes ~60 seconds):

  1. Visit api.census.gov/data/key_signup.html
  2. Fill in your organization name (anything works — "Personal" is fine) and email
  3. Check your email for an activation link from the Census Bureau
  4. Click the link — your key is shown on the activation page
  5. Copy the key and paste it below

Your key is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is sent only to api.census.gov. It's never logged or transmitted to this site.
